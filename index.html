Video editor for iOS

Edit short videos, and keep your footage yours.

JEM Studio is a native iOS editor for short-form video. Import your clips and photos, edit them on a timeline, and export watermark-free. Everything happens on your device.

What it does

  • Import

    Bring in clips and photos through Apple's system picker. JEM Studio only sees the items you choose.

  • Edit on a timeline

    Trim, split, and reorder clips with a timeline built for precise, direct touch.

  • Add text

    Place readable text overlays where you want them.

  • Add audio

    Use your own recordings, add a voiceover, or pick from a small bundled library of public-domain and Creative Commons music.

  • Export clean

    Save finished videos watermark-free straight to your camera roll.

  • Work offline

    No account and no connection required. Editing and export run entirely on your device.

Built on three commitments

Reliability

Autosave and crash recovery mean an interrupted edit is not a lost edit. Losing your work is the failure we care most about preventing.

Honesty

No ads, no trackers, and no upsell maze. The editor is the product, not your attention.

Privacy

No account, no cloud, and no data collection. Your media never leaves your device unless you choose to export or share it. Read the full privacy policy.

JEM Studio is a native app for iPhone, in active development. It has no backend, no advertising, and no analytics that leave your device.
